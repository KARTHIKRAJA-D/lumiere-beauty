About the Artist

Sofia

With over eight years perfecting the craft in New York's most demanding environments — from runway shows during Fashion Week to intimate bridal suites — Sofia Laurent has earned her reputation as one of the city's most sought-after makeup artists.

Trained at the prestigious Christine Valmy International School and certified in airbrush techniques, Sofia believes every face is a canvas with its own story. Her philosophy is simple: enhance, never mask. The result is always you — only luminous.
